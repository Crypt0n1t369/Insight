# Urbit Identity System Deep Dive

## What Is Urbit?
- Persistent digital identity
- Deterministic ID (integer address)
- Personal server (boat)
- No usernames, just numbers

## For Credo
Could inspire our pseudo-anonymous system:
- Your ID = your reputation (no alias)
- Deterministic, portable
- No central registry
- Sparse IDs (not everyone gets one)

## Comparison

| Feature | Urbit | Credo Plan |
|---------|-------|------------|
| Identity | Deterministic (buy) | Earned (contribute) |
| Persistence | Permanent | Tied to engagement |
| Transfer | Sellable | Non-transferable |
| Entry | Buy ($1000+) | Free (wallet) |
