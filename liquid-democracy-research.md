# Liquid Democracy Research for Credo

## What Is Liquid Democracy?
Voting power can be delegated to trusted representatives who can then delegate to others.

### Types

**Delegated Voting (Gitcoin-style)**
- Delegate your vote to an expert
- They vote on your behalf
- Can revoke anytime

**Transitive Delegation**
- A → B → C → D
- D's vote carries A+B+C+D weight
- Creates web of trust

### For Credo
- Youth can delegate to mentors
- Experts accumulate delegated trust
- Can override or revoke anytime
