# Futarchy Research for Credo

## What Is Futarchy?
Robin Hanson's idea: "Vote on values, bet on beliefs"

### How It Works
1. Define what we WANT (values)
2. Create prediction markets on how to GET there
3. Follow the bets that predict success

### Examples
- **Metaculus** - Prediction platform
- **Augur** - Prediction market DAO
- **Polymarket** - Crypto prediction markets

### For Credo
- Branch direction decided by prediction markets
- "Which approach will solve X?"
- Follow the market's wisdom

### Risks
- Market manipulation
- Complexity
- Ethical concerns (betting on outcomes)
