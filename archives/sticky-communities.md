# What Makes Communities "Sticky"

## Research Summary

### 1. Psychological Safety
- Members feel safe to take risks
- No judgment
- Supportive feedback

### 2. Identity
- "I'm a X member"
- Custom profile/status
- Belonging rituals

### 3. Progress
- Visible improvement
- Clear milestones
- Celebrate wins

### 4. Relationships
- Friends made
- Mentors found
- Real connections

### 5. Value
- Learned something
- Contributed something
- Got something useful

---

## Credo Application

### Must Have
- Psychological safety (anonymous + rules)
- Identity (avatar + credibility)
- Progress (badges + tiers)
- Relationships (mentors + peers)
- Value (skills + portfolio)
