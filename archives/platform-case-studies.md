# Platform Case Studies: What Got Youth Right

## Discord

### What Works
- Low barrier (just make account)
- Voice + text + video
- Community servers (not just DMs)
- Role-based access
- Moderation tools

### For Credo
- Integrate Discord-like features?
- Or: own server infrastructure?

---

## Roblox

### What Works
- Creator economy (make games, get paid)
- Safety features (chat filters, parental controls)
- Identity (avatars, not real names)
- Low barrier to creation

### For Credo
- Youth CREATING value
- Not just consuming

---

## TikTok

### What Works
- Algorithmic discovery (quality wins)
- Low production barrier
- Viral = success
- Comments/duet = engagement

### For Credo
- Algorithm should surface quality
- Low barrier to contribute
- Fork/branch virality
