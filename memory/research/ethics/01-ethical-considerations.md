# Ethical Considerations

## AI Representation

### Synthetic Characters
- Is it ethical for AI to represent a person?
- What are the boundaries?
- How do we prevent misuse?

### Consent
- Users must opt-in to representation
- Clear about AI nature
- Easy to disable

## Mental Health

### Not a Replacement
- AI cannot replace human therapists
- Clear disclaimers
- Crisis resources available

### Data Sensitivity
- Audio sessions are deeply personal
- Minimal storage
- Encryption required

## Privacy

### Anonymous But Traceable
- Anonymity protects users
- But can enable bad actors
- Balance needed

### Data Usage
- No selling user data
- Clear opt-in for research
- User control over deletion

## Accessibility

### Must Include
- Screen reader support
- Multiple languages
- Low bandwidth options
- Free tier access
