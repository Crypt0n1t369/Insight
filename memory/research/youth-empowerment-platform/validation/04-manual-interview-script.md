# Validation - Manual Interview Script

## How to Use This

**Option A:** Share this directly with people (text/email)
**Option B:** Use as a script for phone/video calls
**Option C:** Post in communities and collect responses

---

## Message to Send (Copy-Paste)

```
Hey! I'm researching something and would love your honest feedback.

It takes 5 min and you can answer however you want. No right/wrong answers.

Here goes:

1. What's something you want to do or figure out but haven't figured out how yet?

2. Would you use an app where:
- A personal AI gets to know you (your skills, goals)
- Helps you track your growth/journey  
- Matches you with opportunities when they come up
- Everything is private/encrypted

3. How much do you care if apps see your data? (1-10)

4. On a scale of 1-10, how likely are you to try something like that?

5. What's the one thing that would make you NOT use it?

Thanks! 🙏
```

## Quick Analysis

### If you get 10 responses, calculate:

**Engagement Score:** Sum of Q4 (0-10 each) / 10 = __/10

**Privacy Score:** Average of Q3 = __/10

**Usual Patterns:**
- Low Q4 (1-3): Concept rejected
- High Q4 (8-10): Might be too positive - probe deeper
- Q5 common themes: The real objections
