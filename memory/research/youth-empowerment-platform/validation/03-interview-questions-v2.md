## Interview Flow (Conversational)

### Opening (2 min)
"Quick intro — I'm researching something. Not trying to sell you anything. Takes 20 min. Want to be honest — if it's dumb, tell me."

### 1. The Situation (3 min)
"Tell me about yourself — what do you spend time on? Work, school, projects?"
- What does "a typical week" look like?
- What apps do you use most? Why?

### 2. The Struggle (4 min)
"Everyone's got something they want to do or figure out. What's yours?"
- Probe: Goals? Skills they want to use? Things they care about?
- "Ever feel like you have more to offer but don't know how?"
- "What have you tried to figure this out before?"

### 3. The Concept Test (5 min)
"Here's an idea I'm thinking about — tell me if this is dumb or useful:

An app where you create a private vault. You don't even need your real name. An AI assistant gets to know you over time — what you're good at, what you care about, where you're stuck. It helps you:
- Figure out what you actually want
- Track your progress 
- Find opportunities that fit you
- Later: Connect with challenges you can help with

The private part is key — everything encrypted, even the company can't see your data."

### 4. The Reaction (4 min)
- "Does that sound useful? Why/why not?"
- "What's the worst part of that idea?"
- "Would you actually use something like this?"
- "What would make you delete it after one use?"

### 5. Privacy Deep Dive (3 min)
"Quick question on privacy — how much do you care whether apps see your data?"
- "What would you NOT put in an app?"
- "Ever used Incognito mode? For what?"
- "Would you trust an app with your goals/intentions?"

### 6. The AI Question (3 min)
"How do you feel about AI chatbots?"
- "Do you use them? Which ones?"
- "Would you trust an AI to help you figure out your life?"
- "What's the dumbest thing an AI has said to you?"

### 7. The Journey/Growth Question (2 min)
"Do you track your own progress in anything?"
- "Duolingo streaks? Workout logs? Journals?"
- "Does that kind of tracking motivate you or feel like a chore?"
- "How would you feel if an app told you what 'stage' you're at?"

### 8. The Close (2 min)
"Last question — if this app existed, what's the one thing it would need to do for you to open it again tomorrow?"
"On a scale — honestly — would you try this? 1-10. Why that number?"
"Who else should I talk to?"
