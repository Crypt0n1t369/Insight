# Sub-Agent: Business Model & Sustainability Research
## Research Brief

**Agent ID:** subagent-business
**Mission:** Deep dive into monetization, sustainability, and economic design for Credo

---

## CORE CONSTRAINTS (Non-Negotiable)

1. **Youth must be free** - Can't require payment for core functionality
2. **Credibility not purchasable** - No buying reputation  
3. **No selling user data** - Privacy-first
4. **Must be sustainable** - But not at cost to core mission
5. **Pseudo-anonymous** - Can't require ID

---

## RESEARCH QUESTIONS

### Primary Questions
1. What's the right business model for a values-driven platform?
2. How do we avoid the "enshittification" trap (Platform → Advertiser → User)?
3. Can we be free and sustainable?
4. How do we handle the "freemium" tension with credibility?

### Secondary Questions
5. What can we learn from Wikipedia's funding model?
6. How do DAOs generate sustainable income?
7. What grants are available for "moral alignment" research?
8. How do we handle platform migration costs?

## AREAS TO INVESTIGATE

- Freemium (Slack, Notion)

- Credibility decay?
- Portability

### D. Grants & Funding

- Long-Term Future Fund, Protocol Labs
- Government: NSF, EU Horizon

### E. Competitive Moats
- Network effects
- Community (hardest to replicate)
