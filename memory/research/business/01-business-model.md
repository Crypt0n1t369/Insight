# Synthesis Platform Business Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Platform Fees (B2B)
- 5% on funded projects
- Premium features for enterprises
- White-label licensing

### 2. Premium Tiers (B2C)
| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Basic protocols, limited sessions |
| Pro | $9.99/mo | Unlimited sessions, advanced protocols |
| Team | $29.99/mo | Collaboration features |

### 3. API Access
- Researchers can access knowledge graph
- Third-party integrations
- Usage-based pricing

### 4. Grants & Partnerships
- Research institutions
- Mental health organizations
- Government programs

## Cost Structure

### Fixed Costs
- Infrastructure (Vercel, Supabase)
- Domain & certificates
- Development (future team)

### Variable Costs
- AI API calls (scales with usage)
- Audio generation
- Storage (audio files)

## Unit Economics

### Free User
- Cost: $0.02-0.05/month (inference)
- Revenue: $0
- Margin: Negative (but low)

### Pro User  
- Cost: $0.10-0.20/month
- Revenue: $9.99/month
- Margin: ~98%

### Enterprise
- Cost: $50-200/month
- Revenue: Custom
- Margin: ~80%

## Growth Model

### Free → Pro
- In-app upgrades
- Usage-based triggers
- Feature gating

### Individual → Team
- Invite system
- Team dashboard
- Shared knowledge base

### Organic → Enterprise
- Custom deployments
- SLAs
- Dedicated support
