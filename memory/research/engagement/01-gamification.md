# Engagement & Gamification

## Core Principles

### Intrinsic vs Extrinsic Motivation

| Extrinsic (Avoid) | Intrinsic (Build) |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| Points | Autonomy |
| Badges | Mastery |
| Leaderboards | Purpose |
| Streaks | Connection |

### Credo's Approach
- Value-based contribution > points
- Progress visualization > rankings
- Community building > competition

## Engagement Mechanisms

### 1. Progress Tracking
- Session completion
- Protocol mastery
- Contribution history

### 2. Discovery & Unlocks
- New protocols as achievements
- Advanced techniques
- Community roles

### 3. Social Proof
- Reputation tiers
- Contribution highlights
- Endorsements

### 4. Ritual & Rhythm
- Daily check-ins
- Weekly contributions
- Monthly synthesis

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

1. Gaming the system
2. Inflation of rewards
3. Comparison anxiety
4. Addiction loops
