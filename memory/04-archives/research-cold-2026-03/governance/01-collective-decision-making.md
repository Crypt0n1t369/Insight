# Collective Decision-Making Systems

## Voting Mechanisms

### 1. One-Person-One-Vote (1P1V)
- Simple majority
- Pros: Equal weight
- Cons: Vulnerable to sybil

### 2. Quadratic Voting (QV)
- Cost increases quadratically with votes
- Reduces strategic voting
- Used by Gitcoin, Ethereum

### 3. Conviction Voting
- Votes accumulate over time
- Threshold-based passage
- Good for continuous decisions

### 4. Delegated Voting
- Token-weighted delegation
- Liquid democracy
- Used in Compound, MakerDAO

## Consensus Mechanisms

| Mechanism | Speed | Decentralization | Complexity |
|-----------|-------|------------------|------------|
| Majority | Fast | High | Low |
| Quadratic | Medium | High | Medium |
| Delegated | Fast | Medium | Medium |
| DAO | Slow | High | High |

## Application to Credo

### Minimum Viable
- Simple majority for decisions
- Tier-based thresholds (higher trust = more weight)

### Target
- Quadratic voting for proposals
- Reputation-weighted contributions
- Synthetic mediators for representation
