# AI Companion & Digital Representative Systems

## Overview
For Credo, the "digital representative" is core to attracting talent and guiding users.

---

## Existing AI Companion Research

### Replika
- Personal AI companion
- Conversational, emotional support
- 10M+ users
- Controversy: became too romantic/flirty
- Lesson: Clear boundaries needed

### Character.AI
- Character-based conversations
- Multiple personas
- Millions of users
- Heavy engagement (2+ hours/day)
- Lesson: Character variety drives engagement

### Pi (Inflection)
- Personal AI "coach"
- More utilitarian than Replika
- Focus on productivity/wellness
- Lesson: Purposeful AI works

### Glow (情感智能)
- Chinese AI companion
- Emotional support focus
- Character creation
- Lesson: Self-expression drives retention
