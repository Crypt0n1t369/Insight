## Date: 2026-02-28

### Decision: MVP Pillar
**Choice:** Internal Forge (stabilization) — iCOVER/NSDR as the anchor protocol

**Rationale:** 
- Bottom-up physiological regulation is the foundation
- Without stabilization, higher-order work (identity,WOOP) fails
- iCOVER is 60-90 seconds — perfect for MVP audio session
- Aligns with neurobiology research (Doc 1)

---

### Decision: Input Mechanism
**Choice:** AI Chat (Progressive) — Start with simple questionnaire, evolve to chat

**Rationale:**
- MVP: Quick triage (Valence/Arousal slider) → protocol selection
- Future: Natural language understanding
- Lowest friction for v1 while building toward conversational AI

---

### Decision: Audio Output  
**Choice:** TTS Scripts + Binaural — Music deferred

**Rationale:**
- TTS is proven working (Gemini voices)
- Binaural beats add measurable neurological value with low complexity
- Music licensing = legal/complexity trap — skip for MVP
- Can layer in generative music later via APIs

---

### Decision: First Market
**Choice:** Consumer Wellness (test fast)

**Rationale:**
- Lower barrier to entry than military
- Can validate product-market fit quickly
- Build reputation, then pitch military/enterprise
- Aligns with Dan Koe methodology (consumer-focused)
