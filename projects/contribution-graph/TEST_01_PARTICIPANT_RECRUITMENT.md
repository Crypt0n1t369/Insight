## Purpose

Kristaps needs 10 participants for a 30-minute UX research session. This document provides everything ready to use: who to recruit, what to say, how to screen, and what to do after.

**Time required:** ~2–3 hours total (recruitment + sessions spread over 1–2 weeks)
**Target:** 12 recruits → 10 confirmed participants (allow for 2 no-shows)

---

## Who to Recruit

**Target profile (screener criteria):**
- Aged 18–30
- In a transitional moment: recently graduated, between jobs, career uncertain, or feeling stuck/misaligned in current role
- Uses Telegram regularly (required — product is Telegram-based)
- Available for 30 minutes in the next 2 weeks (remote video call or in-person)

**Where to find them:**
- Kristaps' existing network (preferred — faster, more likely to show up)
- JCI Latvia members (age range fits)
- University/RSU contacts (recent graduates)
- Telegram groups for Latvian young professionals

**Do NOT target:** People who are happy and confident in their current career direction. They won't find this useful.

---

## Recruitment Message (Copy-Paste)

Send this exactly as-written via Telegram or direct message:

---

**[Telegram / direct message — send to individual, not group]**

> Hey [name] — I need a favour.
>
> I'm running a short research session for a project I'm working on. It's a 30-minute conversation where I show you a prototype of a self-discovery tool and ask what you think. No preparation needed, no right or wrong answers.
>
> The session is [remote video call or in-person] and takes 30 minutes. I can do [date/time options — pick 3 that work for you].
>
> In exchange: I'll buy you a coffee and share what I find from the research (which might actually be useful for you too).
>
> Interested?

---

**Notes on the message:**
- Keep it casual — this is a personal favour, not a formal study
- The "I'll share what I find" is the real incentive — people are curious about what others think
- Always send to one person at a time (not a group message)
- If they say yes → immediately send the 3 date/time options

---

## Screener Questions (Quick Check)

Before scheduling, verify fit with 3 quick questions (takes 2 minutes):

1. **"What's your current situation — student, working, between jobs?"**
   → Want: student graduating, unemployed, feeling stuck, or restless in current role
   → Avoid: someone thriving in their career

2. **"Do you use Telegram regularly?"**
   → Must be YES — product is Telegram-only for now

3. **"On a scale of 1–10, how clear are you right now about your strongest skills or what you want to be known for?"**
   → Want: 4–6 (some awareness, but not certain)
   → Avoid: 8–10 (too self-aware already)
   → Also avoid: 1–3 (too lost — may need different support)

**Accept if:** 2/3 clearly positive → schedule
**Reject if:** Any clear red flag → thank them politely, move on

## What to Say When They Can't Make It

**If they cancel:**
> No problem — are there other days/times that work for you? I'm flexible.

**If they ghost (no response):** Wait 24 hours, then one follow-up:

> Hey [name] — just checking in. Still want to do the session?

**If no response to follow-up:** Move on, find another participant.

## After Each Session — Log Results Immediately

Copy this into a notes app or spreadsheet right after (takes 2 minutes):

```
Session #[N] | Date: [date] | Participant: [type/description only, not name]

Screener answers:
- Situation: [student/working/between]
- Telegram use: [yes/no]
- Self-awareness (1-10): [score]

Would use? [yes/no/probably/maybe] — verbatim reason:

Day-3 drop-off fear: [what they said — verbatim if possible]

Something unexpected they said: [new objection or insight]

Notes:
```

**Do this within 1 hour of each session** — details fade fast.

---

## Go/No-Go: When to Call the Test

**After 5 sessions:** Do a midpoint check. Are ≥4/5 saying "yes I would use this"?
- If yes → continue recruiting and running sessions
- If no (≥3/5 saying "no" or "probably not") → STOP, reconsider before more sessions

**After 10 sessions:** Final call. Pass criteria:
- ≥7/10 say "yes, I would actually use this"
- ≥5/10 name a specific Day-3 drop-off fear
- ≥3 unique new objections emerged

**If pass:** Proceed to Phase 1 build
**If fail:** Rethink the core consumer value proposition
